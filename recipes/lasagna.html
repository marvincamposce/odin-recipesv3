Lasagna

Description

Prepare to tantalize your taste buds with our Classic Homemade Lasagna recipe! Layer upon layer of rich marinara sauce, perfectly cooked lasagna noodles, creamy ricotta cheese, savory ground beef or italian sausage, and gooey melted mozzarella come together in this comforting dish.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef or italian sausage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 ounces marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Set your oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Cook noodles: Boil lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare Meat Sauce: Cook ground beef (or sausage), onion, and garlic until browned. Add marinara sauce and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Mix Ricotta: Combine ricotta cheese, egg, parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl
  5. Layer Lasagna: In a baking dish, start with a layer of meat sauce, then noodles, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat layers, ending with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  6. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15-20 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  7. Rest and Serve: Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before slicing. Serve warm and enjoy!
